# Status updates and reporting

**Scope:** **Regular** status—what changed, what’s blocked, what’s next—for teams and stakeholders. Not financial accounting. Pair with [`project-briefs.md`](project-briefs.md), [`meetings-and-agendas.md`](meetings-and-agendas.md), and [`impact-and-annual-reporting.md`](../research-&-analysis/impact-and-annual-reporting.md).

## Excerpt

- **RAG** or simple **green/yellow/red** for trajectory—**define** thresholds.
- **Narrative** + **metrics**—story without numbers is vague; numbers without story is noise.
- **Asks** explicit—**decision**, **resource**, or **risk acceptance**.
- **Cadence** predictable—weekly/biweekly, not random pings.
- **Archive** updates—**onboarding** reads history.

## Before sending an update

### Concrete

- **Audience**—exec vs team; **length** budget.
- **Decisions** needed **this** week.

### Meta

- Status theatre (**everything fine**) until it explodes—**yellow early**.

---

## Purpose

Make progress and problems **visible** so help arrives **before** deadlines burn.

---

## 1. Short template

```text
Summary: On track for milestone X (date).
Done: …
Next: …
Blocked: … (owner for unblock)
Asks: …
```

## 2. What not to do

- **Surprise** sponsors with bad news in the **final** hour.

---

## Core idea

Status updates are **control loops**—**signal**, **ask**, **record**.
