# Volunteer coordination

**Scope:** **Coordinating volunteers**—roles, rotas, handovers, appreciation—beyond comms alone. Pair with [`volunteer-comms.md`](../language-&-communication/volunteer-comms.md), [`onboarding-new-team-members.md`](onboarding-new-team-members.md), and [`safeguarding-in-comms.md`](../ethics-&-legal/safeguarding-in-comms.md).

## Excerpt

- **Role descriptions** with **time** and **skills**—clarity beats “help us”.
- **Rotas** and **cover** plans—**no** single points of failure.
- **Handovers**: checklists so shifts **don’t drop** tasks.
- **Recognition** proportionate—**thanks** and **references** (with consent).
- **Safeguarding** rules **front and centre** for roles with vulnerable people.

## Before recruiting volunteers

### Concrete

- **Supervision** model—who is **on call**?
- **Insurance** and **DBS**/background checks per role—**legal** confirms.

### Meta

- Volunteers burn out on **chaos**, not **work**.

## Purpose

Make volunteering **sustainable**—**clear**, **safe**, **appreciated**.

## 1. Rota hygiene

### Bad

```text
Someone will show up.
```

### Good

```text
Shift calendar; backup owner; handover doc at /ops/handover.md
```

## 2. What not to do

- **Guilt** people into extra hours—**ask** and **plan**.

## Core idea

Coordinate volunteers like a **system**: **roles**, **schedules**, **handovers**, **care**.
