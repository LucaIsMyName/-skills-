# Retrospectives

**Scope:** **Team learning** after a milestone—what went well, what to improve, actions. Not blame sessions. Pair with [`meetings-and-agendas.md`](meetings-and-agendas.md), [`decision-logs-and-adrs.md`](decision-logs-and-adrs.md), and [`prioritisation-basics.md`](prioritisation-basics.md).

## Excerpt

- **Psychological safety**—**problems** are systems, not individuals (within safeguarding).
- **Data** from the work: incidents, delays, survey snippets—**not** only vibes.
- **Few** action items with **owners**—better than 20 sticky notes and zero change.
- **Rotate** facilitation—**share** power.
- **Follow up** next retro—did we actually improve?

## Before the retro

### Concrete

- **Scope**: which project/timebox?
- **Participants** who did the work.

### Meta

- Retros without **follow-through** train cynicism.

---

## Purpose

Turn experience into **better defaults**—**visible** improvements, not ritual.

---

## 1. Simple format

- What helped? What hurt? What experiments next?

### Bad

```text
Vent for 60 minutes, no actions.
```

### Good

```text
30m retro → pick 2 experiments → owners → calendar check-in
```

## 2. What not to do

- **Public** blame or **breaking** confidentiality.

---

## Core idea

Retros **pay rent** with **changed behaviour**—otherwise they’re theatre.
