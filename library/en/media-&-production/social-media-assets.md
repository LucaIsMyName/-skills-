# Social media assets

**Scope:** **Sizing** and **exporting** for major platforms—safe zones, video specs, accessibility. Not growth hacking. Pair with [`headline-writing.md`](../language-&-communication/headline-writing.md), [`file-naming-and-organising.md`](file-naming-and-organising.md), and [`motion-and-micro-interactions.md`](../design/motion-and-micro-interactions.md).

## Excerpt

- **Specs change**—**verify** current platform docs before a major launch.
- **Safe zones** for UI overlays—keep **faces** and **logos** clear.
- **Alt text** on images; **captions** on video.
- **Loop** length and **hook** in first seconds for short video.
- **Brand** consistency via **templates**—not one-off chaos.

## Before exporting

### Concrete

- **Primary** platforms this month; **dark-on-light** variants if needed.
- **Compression** trade-offs—**test** on a phone.

### Meta

- **Every** resize is a chance to **crop truth**—**don’t**.

---

## Purpose

Ship assets that **fit**, **read**, and **stay on-brand**—without last-minute re-exports.

---

## 1. Practical pattern

- Master design in **vector** or **large raster**; export **per** spec from a checklist.

### Bad

```text
One giant square exported everywhere—heads cropped on Stories.
```

### Good

```text
Separate crops per ratio; safe-area guides; named exports.
```

## 2. What not to do

- **Tiny** text in thumbnails—**illegible** on mobile.

---

## Core idea

Social assets are **constraints engineering**—**specs** first, **creativity** inside.
