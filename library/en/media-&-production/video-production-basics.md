# Video production basics

**Scope:** **Short-form** video for web/social—planning, lighting, audio, edit handoff. Not cinema. Pair with [`subtitles-and-captions.md`](subtitles-and-captions.md), [`file-naming-and-organising.md`](file-naming-and-organising.md), and [`live-events-and-streaming.md`](live-events-and-streaming.md).

## Excerpt

- **Audio first**—bad sound kills trust faster than soft video.
- **Lighting**: soft, even; **avoid** harsh shadows on faces.
- **B-roll** for coverage and **honest** context.
- **Formats**: **vertical** vs **horizontal** per platform—**plan**.
- **Legal**: music **licensed**; **locations** permitted.

## Before filming

### Concrete

- **Script** or outline; **run-of-show**; **backup** mic.
- **Consent** on camera for identifiable people.

### Meta

- **Edit** is where the story becomes **honest** or **misleading**.

---

## Purpose

Ship **clear** video that people can **hear**, **see**, and **trust**.

---

## 1. Minimum kit

- Shotgun or lav mic; tripod; light bounce or softbox if indoors.

### Bad

```text
Built-in laptop mic across the room.
```

### Good

```text
Lav on speaker; 10s room tone; clap sync if multicam.
```

## 2. What not to do

- **Misleading** cuts that invent reactions.

---

## Core idea

Video quality is **audio + light + edit discipline**—**gear** second.
